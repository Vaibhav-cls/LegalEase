Are you a legal service provider?

Benefits of being in our platform:

  • After completing a service you will get an incentive based on your client's feedback.
  • With each good review you will rank up in our platform.
  • You will get badges as a part of recognition which will motivate you towards your good work.
  • The higher will be your rank, clients will contact you frequently.

Important note for our clients

Do's

  • Recommend our platform to your friends and family members.
  • After getting a service do not forget to submit review about the provider. It will help us and the provider as well.
  • Communicate your legal needs and expectations clearly to your chosen service provider.
  • Be mindful of agreed-upon deadlines and respond promptly to requests from your service provider.
  • Keep your account information secure and report any suspicious activity immediately.

Don'ts

  • Avoid providing false or misleading information about your case or personal details.
  • Maintain professionalism and respect in all communications with service providers.
  • Do not share sensitive legal information in public areas of the platform.
  • Avoid making side arrangements with providers outside the platform to ensure accountability and security.
